Butter Milk Fried Chicken
=========================

+-----------------------+------------------------+------------------+-------------+
| Prep Time: 15 minutes | Marinate Time: 6 hours | Cook: 35 minutes | Servings: 8 |
+-----------------------+------------------------+------------------+-------------+

Ingredients
-----------

Marinade
^^^^^^^^

-  3 1/2 pound (1.6kg) chicken, cut in 8 pieces
-  1 tsp salt
-  1 tsp black pepper
-  1 tsp paprika
-  1/4 tsp cayenne
-  1/2 tsp white pepper
-  1 tsp ground dried herbs (oregano, thyme, rosemary, sage)
-  2 cups buttermilk  (substitute 1 tablespoon of cream of tartar and 2 cups regular milk)

Seasoned flour
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

-  2 cups flour
-  1 tsp salt
-  1/2 tsp paprika
-  1/4 tsp cayenne
-  1/2 tsp white pepper
-  1/2 tsp garlic powder
-  1/2 tsp onion powder

Directions
----------

1. Toss together chicken pieces, black pepper, salt, paprika, rosemary,
   thyme, oregano, sage, white pepper, and cayenne in a large bowl to
   coat.
2. Stir in buttermilk until chicken is coated. Refrigerate for 6 hours.
3. Combine flour, salt, paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, white pepper,
   and onion powder in a large shallow dish.
4. Remove chicken from the buttermilk and dredge each piece in the
   seasoned flour. Shake off any excess and transfer to a plate.
5. Heat oil (preferably peanut oil) in a large Dutch oven to 350°F
   (175°C). Add all the chicken to the pan and cook for 10 minutes.
6. Turn chicken pieces and cook for another 10-15 minutes.
7. Remove chicken from the oil and transfer to a cooling rack set over a
   paper towel lined baking sheet. Let sit for 10 minutes before
   serving. Goes well with homemade `French Fries <#french-fries>`__.
