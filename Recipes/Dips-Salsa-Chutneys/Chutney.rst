Chutney
=======

Ingredients
-----------

- 1 cup cilantro, leaves
- 2 green chili
- 2 tablespoon Ginger
- 2 tablespoon lemon juice, fresh
- Sea salt

Directions
----------

1. Grind cilantro leaves, ginger, and green chili together
2. If the paste is too thick add a little water
3. Then add lemon juice and salt. Mix well.
