Orinara Yaki Dipping Sauce
==========================

Ingredients
-----------

-  1 tablespoon mirin
-  1 tablespoon soy sauce
-  1 cold water
-  1 tablespoon water
-  1 teaspoon chili sauce (sambal oelek)
-  1 teaspoon minced garlic

Directions
----------

1. Whisk all ingredients together in small bowl.
