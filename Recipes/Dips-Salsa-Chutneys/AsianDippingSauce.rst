Asian Dipping Sauce
===================

Ingredients
-----------

-  2 tablespoons soy sauce
-  1 scallion, sliced thin
-  1 tablespoon water
-  2 teaspoons rice vinegar
-  1 teaspoon honey
-  1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
-  Pinch red pepper flakes

Directions
----------

1. Whisk all ingredients together in small bowl.
