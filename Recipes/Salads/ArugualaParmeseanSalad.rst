Aruguala Parmesean Salad
========================

Ingredients
-----------

- 4 cups Aruguala
- 1 Lemon
- 1/4 cup Olive oil
- pinch of salt
- 2 oz Parmesean Cheese Grated (about 1/3 cup)

Directions
----------

1. Place arugula in a bowl and drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and
   squeeze a lemon over top.  Cover in parmsean.
