Honey Vinaigrette
=================

Ingredients
-----------

- 1/4 cup white wine or red wine vinegar
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil or grapeseed oil
- 2 tablespoons Honey
- 1 teaspoons dijon mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Directions
----------

1. Place all vinaigrette ingredients in a sealed jar or container and shake
   aggressively.
