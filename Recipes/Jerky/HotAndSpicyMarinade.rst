Hot and Spicy Marinade
======================

Ingredients
-----------

-  1 cup Soy sauce
-  1 tablespoons Molasses
-  2 teaspoons Liquid smoke
-  2 teaspoons Black pepper
-  2 teaspoons Garlic powder
-  2 teaspoons Onion powder
-  1 teaspoons Red pepper flakes
-  1 teaspoons Tabasco
-  1/2 teaspoons Cayenne pepper
