Tequila Beef Jerky
==================

Ingredients
-----------

-  4 tablespoons Tequila
-  2 tablespoons lime juice (freshly squeezed)
-  1 teaspoons cayenne pepper
-  1 teaspoons garlic powder
-  1 teaspoons onion powder
-  1 teaspoons kosher salt
-  2 teaspoons cracked black pepper
-  1/2 cup cold water
