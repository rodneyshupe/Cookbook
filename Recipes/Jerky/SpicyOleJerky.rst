Spicy Ole Jerky
===============

Ingredients
-----------

-  4 tablespoons water
-  4 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
-  2 teaspoons salt
-  2 teaspoons sugar
-  4 garlic cloves, pressed
-  1/2 teaspoons hot sauce
-  2 teaspoons ground cumin
-  2 teaspoons ground coriander
-  1 tablespoons chili powder
