Mustard Sauce
=============

Ingredients
-----------

- 1/4 cup (60 grams) Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons whole grain mustard
- 1 1/2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 1/4 teaspoon chili flakes
- 1 lemon, juiced
- kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
